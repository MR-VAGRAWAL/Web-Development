Reciepe Of Tea

  1. Boil Three Cups Of Milk And Half Cup Of Water
  2. Add 2 Spoons Of Tea Leaves And 1 Spoon Sugar
  3. You Can Also Add Some Of The Following Ingridients According To Your Taste:-

    • Ginger
    • Clove
    • Black Peeper
    • Tea Masala
  4. Boil It For Few Minutes
  5. Filter The Tea In Cups
  6. Enjoy It With Your Favourite Snacks
